Booking FAQ

Find answers to common questions about the booking process

What is your payment policy?

We require a 30% deposit to confirm your booking. The remaining balance is due at your arrival date. Payment can be made by credit card, bank transfer or Paypal.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 30 days before arrival with full deposit refund. Cancellations between 30 and 14 days before arrival forfeit the deposit. Cancellations less than 14 days before arrival are charged the full amount.

Can I modify my booking after confirmation?

Yes, modifications are possible subject to availability. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to change your dates or add services. Changes made less than 14 days before arrival may incur additional charges.
