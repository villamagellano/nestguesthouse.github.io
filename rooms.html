Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our accommodations

What is the check-in/check-out time?

Check-in is from 4:00 PM to 12:00 PM, and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.

Are pets allowed?

We welcome pets upon request. A pet fee of €20 per stay applies. Please inform us at the time of booking.

Is there a minimum stay requirement?

During high season (June-September), we require a minimum stay of 3 nights. During the rest of the year, the minimum stay is 2 nights. For holiday periods, different minimums may apply.

How do I get to Villa Magellano?

The nearest highway exits are Peschiera del Garda (A4) and Verona Sud (A12). The nearest airports are Verona (12 km) and Bergamo (30 km). We can arrange airport transfers for an additional fee, or you can rent a car. Detailed directions will be provided upon booking confirmation.
