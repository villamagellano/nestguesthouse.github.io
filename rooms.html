Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our accommodations

What is the check-in/check-out time?

Check-in is from 3:00 PM to 8:00 PM, and check-out is by 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request, subject to availability.

Is breakfast included?

Yes, a complimentary continental breakfast with local products is included with your stay. We also offer enhanced breakfast options for an additional fee.

Are pets allowed?

We welcome well-behaved pets in specific trulli houses (Trullo Giardino only) with prior arrangement. A pet fee of €25 per stay applies. Please inform us at the time of booking.

Is there a minimum stay requirement?

During high season (June-September), we require a minimum stay of 3 nights. During the rest of the year, the minimum stay is 2 nights. For holiday periods, different minimums may apply.

How do I get to Yours Trulli?

The nearest airports are Bari (70 km) and Brindisi (65 km). We can arrange airport transfers for an additional fee, or you can rent a car. Detailed directions will be provided upon booking confirmation.
