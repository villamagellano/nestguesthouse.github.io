Our Journey

The story of Yours Trulli began in 2010 when Maria and Antonio Rossi, enchanted by the distinctive beauty of trulli architecture, purchased a cluster of abandoned trulli houses nestled in the countryside near Alberobello.

Having grown up in Puglia, the couple had a deep connection to the region's cultural heritage. They envisioned bringing these historic structures back to life while preserving their authenticity and sharing the magic of trulli living with travelers from around the world.

After three years of careful restoration, working with local artisans who understood the traditional building techniques, Yours Trulli welcomed its first guests in 2013. Today, our property stands as a testament to the perfect harmony between historical preservation and contemporary comfort.
