    Welcome to Yours Trulli

    A unique blend of history, luxury, and natural beauty

    Nestled in the enchanting countryside of the southern Garda Lake, our carefully restored trulli houses offer an extraordinary stay experience. Our Villa combines authentic architecture with contemporary comfort, creating the perfect backdrop for your Italian getaway.

    Discover our story

    Authentic Trulli Experience

    Our trulli houses are meticulously restored to preserve their authentic charm while incorporating modern amenities for your comfort. Experience the unique architecture and history of these UNESCO-protected dwellings that have defined the Puglia landscape for centuries.

    Immersed in Nature

    Surrounded by ancient olive groves, vineyards, and the gentle rolling hills of the Valle d'Itria, our property offers an idyllic retreat into nature. Enjoy panoramic views, peaceful gardens, and the refreshing private pool that perfectly complements the Pugliese sun.
